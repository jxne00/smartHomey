About SmartHomey

This web application was created by June Pang
as part of the Mid-Term Coursework for the "CM2040 Databases, Networks and the Web" module.

This application is designed to provide easy monitoring and control of devices in a smart home.


Functions of SmartHomey include:

  • Adding a device
  • Viewing all added devices
  • Updating a device
  • Deleting a device
  • Searching for an added device
